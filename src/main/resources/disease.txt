black spot|Plant resistant varieties, ensure good air circulation, avoid overhead watering|Remove and destroy infected leaves|Use Chlorothalonil, Mancozeb, or Copper fungicides

verticillium wilt|Plant resistant/tolerant varieties, rotate crops, and avoid planting in infested soil|solarize soil to reduce inoculum|Use Triazole fungicides in controlled manner

cedar-apple rust|remove nearby junipers|Prune and destroy galls on cedar/juniper hosts|Use Myclobutanil or Propiconazole during apple bloom

pierce's disease|Remove nearby host plants (alfalfa, oleander, citrus) that harbor sharpshooters|Once vines are infected, they cannot be cured; severely diseased vines are usually removed to prevent spread|Use insecticides : Imidacloprid or Pyrethroids.
